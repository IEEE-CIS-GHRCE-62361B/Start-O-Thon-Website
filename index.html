Start-o-Thon

Start-O-Thon is a 15-day Start-Up hackathon where aspiring entrepreneurs will foster open innovation and cater solutions and bring out the start-up ideas. Start-O-Thon is an exciting opportunity for all to win and develop socially impactful startup ideas for different domains.

How much does it cost to take part in this program?
Start-O-Thon is an absolutely free hackathon, you can participate by applying on Devfolio.

Why should we participate in Start-O-Thon?
Start-O-Thon is a start-up hackathon that will be held on a virtual platform, which means it provides you an opportunity to participate directly from your home, with no fuss of going anywhere. So just ideate, innovate and implement and win prizes up to Rs. 45,000/-.
What is the eligibility?
All students are allowed to participate in this hackathon.
How do we contact Start-o-thon organisers?
